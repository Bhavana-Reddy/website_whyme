About India Fastener

India Fastener is a Business Aggregator for Fasteners & provides Std & Weight Count Solution.
India Fastener has been started off with an initiative to link the ever growing field of technology with the world of fasteners. We have started off with an mobile solutions that facilitates you while handling your day to day weigh count issues. We already have the app for Android and iOS.
In all our efforts the core focus is innovation, and improving products for the end users. We strive extra mile to make sure you get the best and the solution helps you to focus on your core business.
We hope you will like our application. We are open to feedback and suggests, to improve this application.
